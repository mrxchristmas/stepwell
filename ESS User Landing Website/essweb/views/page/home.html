ESS is a design build company manufacturing process equipment and automated skid for various industries. ESS started its operations in year 2000 as a filter pressure vessel manufacturer and has slowly grown as a full custom skid fabricator including skids for sanitary industry. We work directly with clients and / or engineering firms from various stages of design, fabrication and testing to provide the quality build. ESS Corp offers products and services through a world-wide network of distributors and industrial sales organizations, as well as through direct sales to end users. ESS Corp can also offers a variety of engineered systems with complete "turn-key" solutions based on customer requirement and specification. Apart from full team of Mechanical Designers, Process Engineers and Validation Experts, we also work with range of supplier and partners that helps to integrate their solution in mechanical, process, electrical, structural and control systems design. Our long-time experience in pressure vessels and piping systems design allows us to provide our customers with high quality products, at cost saving prices and exceptional service. ESS can design custom equipment as per client need and following industry best practices for design, fabrication and commissioning. We can execute projects with exceptional quality control and project management starting from specification development to fabrication to commissioning/testing and generating quality turnover packages Our team has constituted of Project Managers, Engineers, Designers, Quality Assurance Specialists and fabricators (that includes welders, fitter, machinist, testers and general helpers). For the convenience of our clients, we stock some finished units and spare parts, as well as consumables, required for everyday use of our products. Apart from pressure vessels fabrication, we fabricate in- house structural fabrication of frames and platforms, pipe welding, rolling and machined parts.
